The Best Movies According to Sai Prakash

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

1. Silence Of the Lambs

FBI trainee Clarice hunts serial killer Buffalo Bill, aided by brilliant, terrifying Hannibal Lecter, blending psychology, horror, and suspense classic.

2.Salaar

A violent, loyal man lives in hiding until his past drags him back into Khansaar, a brutal kingdom ruled by power, betrayal, and bloodlines. His childhood bond with a future king turns into a test of loyalty versus ambition. Politics, gang warfare, and revenge collide. The first part is world-building heavy—slow but deliberate—setting up an explosive continuation.

3.Bahubali

A prince discovers his royal past, challenges tyranny, avenges betrayal, and reclaims Mahishmati through sacrifice, courage, and destiny fulfilled honor.
